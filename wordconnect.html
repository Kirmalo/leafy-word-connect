How to Play
Leafy Word Connect
👆
Step 1 — Swipe the Letters
Press and hold on any letter, then drag your finger across the circle to connect letters in order. You can go in any direction.
🔤
Step 2 — Form a Word
The letters you select appear in the bar above the wheel. It glows green when you've made a valid word. Lift your finger to submit.
📋
Step 3 — Fill the Grid
Each word fills its spot in the crossword grid above. Find all the words to complete the level. The grid shows you the shape of each word.
⭐
Bonus Words
You can find extra words not in the grid for bonus coins! Any real word earns you something. The more letters, the bigger the reward.
💡
Stuck? Use Power-Ups
💡 Hint highlights a letter. 🔀 Shuffle rearranges the wheel. 🔮 Reveal shows a whole word. Tap the buttons below the wheel.
